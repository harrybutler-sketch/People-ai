    Relevance Scoring Guide

    The system analyzes text using a weights-based scoring formula:

    Low Relevance (Score < 15): Basic AI mentions without actionable sourcing indicators.

    Medium Relevance (Score 15 - 30): Company is scaling model development, or discussing datasets.

    High Relevance (Score > 30): Direct intent to source data, scale annotation projects, or complaints about synthetic accuracy.
